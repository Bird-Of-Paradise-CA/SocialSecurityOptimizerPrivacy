Privacy Policy: Social Security Optimizer

Effective Date: 11/01/2025

1. Introduction

Social Security Optimizer (“the App”) is a financial planning tool designed to help users model retirement income strategies based on Social Security benefits. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or Social Security numbers.

The App may collect:

  • Non-personal usage data (e.g., app interactions, feature usage)
  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version) for performance optimization

3. Data Usage

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage trends and performance

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store data on external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 4 and up and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there are no user accounts or data access requests. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be posted within the app and on our website.
